Happy Teacher's Day

September 5

History

Teachers' Day is a special day dedicated to honoring and appreciating the invaluable contributions of teachers and educators in society. It is celebrated in various countries around the world on different dates, but the idea behind it remains the same: to recognize and express gratitude to teachers for their tireless efforts in educating and shaping the future of students.
